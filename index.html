About Synesthesia Simulator

Synesthesia is a neurological phenomenon where stimulation of one sensory pathway leads to automatic experiences in a second sensory pathway. This simulator demonstrates the concept by mapping keyboard inputs to both visual and auditory experiences.

Each key press generates a unique sound frequency and corresponding visual representation. Lower frequencies produce warmer colors and larger shapes, while higher frequencies create cooler colors and smaller forms.
